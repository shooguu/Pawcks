Founded in 2019, Pawcks was made to provide the best experience for shopping socks for dogs. Our socks are made with 100% cotton with love and care. We try to make sure our socks can fit any size dogs with comfort. We try to minimize irritation by putting an extra layer of cotton inside the socks so the dogs can wear these for days.
