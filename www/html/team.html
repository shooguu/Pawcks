Founder
Ron Swanson is the founder and CEO of the company. He decided to give this company a go when he realized that his dogs would slip a lot around his house. He thought by making anti slip socks for dogs, they wouldn't have this problem anymore

Vice President
His best friend Amy took the position as vice president. They have known each other since grade school, graduated from the same college with the same degree.

The Shoe Shine
